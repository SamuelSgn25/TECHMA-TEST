## 💬 COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

**Q: Why not use AWS/Google Cloud instead of self-hosting?**
A: "That's a great question! Our approach gives you complete control and privacy. For enterprise users, we're planning cloud deployment options, but the open-source core stays self-hostable."

**Q: How does this handle privacy differently than ChatGPT?**
A: "With ChatGPT, your files and data get sent to OpenAI's servers and used for training. With Drive AI, everything runs on your own server. Your data never leaves your machine."

**Q: What's the performance impact for 50GB files?**
A: "50GB is for the upload capability. Processing happens in background — users don't wait. For example, transcribing a 10-hour video takes about 30-60 minutes depending on your CPU."

**Q: Is this production-ready?**
A: "The code is production-ready, but I'd recommend testing in your specific use case first. We're actively developing and welcome feedback."

**Q: How does it compare to [Competitor]?**
A: "They solve different problems. [Competitor] is better for X, we're better for Y. The real question is what fits YOUR use case best."

**Q: What's your monetization plan?**
A: "We're keeping the core open source forever. We might offer hosted SaaS version later, but self-hosting remains free."

**Q: Can I use this commercially?**
A: "Yes! MIT license means you can use it for commercial purposes, including using it internally for your business."
